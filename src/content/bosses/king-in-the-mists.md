## Full Fight Guide

The King in the Mists is an optional Act 1 boss, found by activating three Ritual Altars in his
arena, a black cloud blocks the path to him until all three are complete. He's a reasonable
early check on your build's ability to handle a real two-phase fight, and the permanent Spirit
reward from your first kill is worth picking up even if you're not planning to fully clear
optional content on every character.

If your build isn't quite ready for him at the level you encounter him, PoE2's multiplayer
support lets you bring in help rather than forcing a solo attempt.
