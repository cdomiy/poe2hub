## Full Fight Guide

Xesht, We That Are One is the pinnacle boss of the Breach mechanic, fought in the Twisted Domain.
Getting there requires collecting 300 Breach Splinters, which combine into a Breachstone, place
it into the Realmgate (always located next to the Ziggurat Refuge on your Atlas) to open the
portal. Inside, you fight through waves of Breach monsters to extend a countdown timer before
reaching the boss arena itself.

The fight has 4 difficulty levels, unlocked and scaled through the Breach-specific Atlas Passive
Tree, higher difficulty means significantly more boss health, but a much better loot table in
return.

### Gearing checklist before you attempt this

- A **Freeze charm** specifically counters Tul's icicle summons
- **25%+ movement speed** on boots makes the dodge timing across every phase meaningfully easier
- Layered defenses (Armour, Block, or Energy Shield) plus a source of Life Leech or regeneration
  help you survive extended fights where multiple mechanics overlap
- Remember pinnacle bosses **cannot be paused**, if you need to step away mid-fight, log out
  rather than pausing, and be ready to resume immediately when you log back in
