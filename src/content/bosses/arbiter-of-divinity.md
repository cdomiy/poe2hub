## Full Fight Guide

The Arbiter of Divinity replaced the Arbiter of Ash as PoE2's top pinnacle boss in the 0.5.0
Return of the Ancients update. It lives in the Origin Tower at the center of the Precursor
Fortress, and you can't reach it cold, you need to defeat the Arbiter of Ash first, then
assemble an Origin Core from Origin Spark and Origin Cradle keys dropped by two Citadel bosses.

Your first encounter happens automatically through the Origins of Divinity questline, at
reduced difficulty. Every subsequent fight is the full pinnacle version, unlocked the normal
way through Atlas keys.

### Damage profile

The Arbiter deals primarily Fire and Lightning damage, and is resistant to all elements plus
extremely resistant to Physical damage, cap your elemental resistances before attempting this
fight rather than leaning on armour alone.

### Difficulty scaling

Like other 0.5 pinnacle invitations, this fight has a difficulty slider (0-4) that trades
respawn count for better rewards: five respawns at Difficulty 0 down to a single attempt at
Difficulty 4. If your Arbiter of Ash kill needed multiple respawns, don't jump straight to
higher Divinity difficulties, the phase 2 clone sequence gets meaningfully less forgiving as
your respawn budget shrinks.
