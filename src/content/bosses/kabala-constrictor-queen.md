## Full Fight Guide

Kabala, Constrictor Queen is an optional Act 2 boss found hidden underground in the Lost City of
Keth, beneath a marked checkpoint area. She deals Chaos and Physical damage and leaves large
poison zones that restrict your movement options throughout the fight. Positioning matters more
here than in most optional Act 2 encounters.

She's entirely skippable if you're rushing the campaign, but the Book of Specialization reward
is genuinely useful for any build planning to run two distinct weapon sets, worth the detour on
a first playthrough.
