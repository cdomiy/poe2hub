## Full Fight Guide

Kabala, Constrictor Queen is an optional Act 2 boss found hidden underground in the Lost City of Keth, beneath a marked checkpoint area. She deals Chaos and Physical damage and leaves large poison zones that restrict your movement options throughout the fight. Positioning matters more here than in most optional Act 2 encounters.

To find Kabala, explore Keth and look for the Venom Pit — a subterranean arena near a checkpoint. The spawn locations seem to be on the corners of a square in the middle of the area. If you find a head-shaped tile in the upper portion of the zone, Kabala will always spawn either to the left or right of it [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/campaign-layout-act-2).

She is entirely skippable if you are rushing the campaign, but the Book of Specialization reward is genuinely useful for any build planning to run two distinct weapon sets — worth the detour on a first playthrough [(ethugamer.com)](https://ethugamer.com/path-of-exile-2/path-of-exile-2-act-2-secrets-hidden-rewards-complete-exploration-guide-and-walkthrough/).

## Phase-by-Phase Breakdown

### Baseline Phase (100%–90% Health)

The fight opens with Kabala using a mix of quick melee attacks and wind-based abilities. The arena has a dangerous central area that you should avoid lingering in — the outer corridors are your best friend for positioning [(blockshare.it)](https://blockshare.it/blogs/28259/U4GM-What-to-Do-Against-Kabala-in-POE-2-Guide?lang=ro_ro).

**Key Abilities:**

- **Wind Shard** — A fast close-range wind projectile. Requires Kabala to be in melee range, so dodging to the side or simply repositioning out of range handles it easily.
- **Tail Swipe** — A wide circular sweep with significant reach. Keep moving during the animation rather than standing still near her. This attack cannot be dodge-rolled through — you must be outside its radius [(poe2db.tw)](https://poe2db.tw/fr/Kabala%2C_Constrictor_Queen).
- **Dagger Slashes** — Quick melee swings in front of her. Strafe laterally to avoid the forward hitbox.
- **Off-hand Explosive Projectiles** — Small thrown explosives that detonate on impact. The damage is manageable individually but adds up if you are standing still.

The broken pottery around the arena edges is not decoration — it is Kabala's vending machine for skeleton cobras later in the fight. Be aware of these positions so you can react quickly when Bone Snakes spawn.

### 90% Health — Bone Snakes

Kabala channels her own blood into jars placed around the arena, summoning Bone Snakes (skeleton cobras) that attack you. These are not individually tanky, but they are effective at being annoying — blocking pathing, poking you down, and messing up your dodge timings [(blockshare.it)](https://blockshare.it/blogs/28259/U4GM-What-to-Do-Against-Kabala-in-POE-2-Guide?lang=ro_ro).

**Strategy:** Clear the snakes promptly rather than ignoring them. Use the outer corridors like a choke point — back into one, let the snakes line up, and sweep them with AoE. Treating add management as a priority rather than an afterthought is what separates successful attempts from wipes. People who try to "just finish the boss" end up stuck, flasking, and panicking when the snakes stack alongside Kabala's direct attacks.

### 80% Health — Explosive Nova

Kabala spins in a circle, releasing a large spray of explosive projectiles in a nova pattern. The projectiles detonate shortly after landing, creating a delayed double-tap pattern where the initial impact zone and the subsequent explosion zone are both dangerous.

**Strategy:** Get outside the nova's radius during the spin animation. Do not try to tank through the delayed detonations — the combined damage from multiple projectiles can kill even well-geared characters. If you are in melee range when the spin begins, immediately dodge-roll outward and keep moving until the detonations finish.

### 70% Health — The Vulnerability Wipe (Critical Mechanic)

This is the fight's defining mechanic and the reason most players die to Kabala. She dives into the sand and creates Bone Walls at every arena entrance except one, then charges a massive arena-wide explosion while applying a **20-second Vulnerability debuff** that increases physical damage taken by 40% and gives 50% chance to bleed on hit [(poe2db.tw)](https://poe2db.tw/fr/Kabala%2C_Constrictor_Queen).

**Strategy:** The moment Kabala burrows, stop attacking and locate the single open entrance immediately. Move through the gap before the explosion resolves. This is a pure execution check — there is no way to tank the combination of arena-wide damage plus the Vulnerability debuff. The gap is always exactly one entrance; learn the arena layout before the fight so you can quickly identify which wall has the opening [(blockshare.it)](https://blockshare.it/blogs/28259/U4GM-What-to-Do-Against-Kabala-in-POE-2-Guide?lang=ro_ro).

After the explosion, Kabala re-emerges and the fight continues with her baseline abilities plus any remaining Bone Snakes. The Vulnerability debuff persists for 20 seconds, so play cautiously during this window — even her basic attacks become dangerous with 40% increased physical damage taken.

## Preparation & Gear Checklist

- **Chaos Resistance** — Kabala deals Physical and Chaos damage. While Chaos resistance is harder to stack early in Act 2, any amount helps. Consider equipping gear with Chaos resistance suffixes before the fight.
- **Physical Damage Mitigation** — Armour and flat physical damage reduction are valuable since most of Kabala's attacks deal physical damage. The Vulnerability debuff makes physical mitigation even more important during the 20-second window after the 70% mechanic.
- **Movement Speed** — Kabala's fight is a movement and decision encounter, not a DPS check. Boots with increased movement speed make the 70% Bone Wall gap check significantly easier [(blockshare.it)](https://blockshare.it/blogs/28259/U4GM-What-to-Do-Against-Kabala-in-POE-2-Guide?lang=ro_ro).
- **AoE Damage** — Essential for clearing Bone Snake spawns efficiently. Builds without AoE capability will struggle with add management.
- **Level 25+ recommended** — If you are underleveled, farm experience in Keth (the zone has excellent XP) before attempting the boss.
- **Flask Preparation** — Roll flasks so they actually provide meaningful recovery. A flask that removes bleeding is especially valuable during the Vulnerability debuff window.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Standing still during Tail Swipe or the explosive nova instead of repositioning.** Both abilities punish stationary play. Develop a habit of constant lateral movement during Kabala's attack windows.

2. **Failing to locate the single open Bone Wall gap before the arena-wide explosion at 70% health.** This is the wipe mechanic. The moment Kabala burrows, your only priority is finding the gap and moving through it. Stop attacking, stop channeling, and run.

3. **Letting Bone Snake summons stack up while focused purely on the boss.** The snakes are individually weak but collectively dangerous. They block your escape routes during the 70% mechanic and add chip damage that depletes your flask charges before the real threats appear.

4. **Tanking through the Vulnerability debuff window.** After surviving the 70% explosion, you have 20 seconds of 40% increased physical damage taken. Do not play aggressively during this window — focus on survival until the debuff expires.

5. **Lingering in the arena center.** The middle of the arena is the most dangerous position throughout the entire fight. Use the outer corridors for positioning and retreat to them when new mechanics activate.

## Tips for Specific Builds

### Melee / Close-Range Builds
Melee characters must adopt a "touch, touch, leave" pattern against Kabala. Tap in for damage during her recovery windows, then reset your position. The Tail Swipe and Explosive Nova both punish extended melee uptime. During the 70% mechanic, melee players have an advantage — you are already close to the arena edges, making it faster to reach the Bone Wall gap. Prioritize clearing Bone Snakes with wide melee sweeps rather than single-target attacks.

### Ranged / Projectile Builds
Ranged builds have a natural advantage in this fight since most of Kabala's abilities require her to be in close proximity to connect. Maintain maximum range and kite along the outer corridors. The 70% Bone Wall gap check is slightly harder for ranged players who tend to position at the arena's center-back — train yourself to fight from the edges. Use piercing or chaining projectiles to hit Kabala and Bone Snakes simultaneously.

### Spell / Caster Builds
Casters with strong AoE spells excel at Bone Snake clearance, which is the primary add-management challenge. However, Kabala's fight requires more movement than typical Act 2 encounters, so avoid stationary channeling skills. Instant-cast or quick-animation spells let you maintain damage output while continuously repositioning. Cold-based spells are particularly effective since they can slow the Bone Snakes, giving you more time to react to Kabala's direct attacks.

### Summoner / Minion Builds
Minion builds can delegate Bone Snake clearing to their army, freeing you to focus on Kabala's mechanics. Position your minions between yourself and the boss to create a buffer zone. The 70% Vulnerability mechanic is the main threat — your minions cannot help you escape the arena-wide explosion, so you must personally find the gap. Consider using a movement skill to ensure you can reach the exit quickly when the mechanic triggers.
