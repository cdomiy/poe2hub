## Full Fight Guide

Atziri, the Red Queen is the pinnacle boss of Atziri's Temple (the Fate of the Vaal mechanic).
Reaching her requires building the Temple up to at least Area Level 75, defeating Xipocado, the
Royal Architect (who spawns on a random tile in your layout), and using his console to place the
Royal Access Chamber. Path your temple layout to connect to that chamber, and Atziri's door
unlocks.

There's no mid-fight resurrection mechanic here, if you fail, your entire path and unlocked
rooms reset and you need to rebuild the route from scratch. Because of this, it's worth
maximizing your level, gear, and Ascendancy points before attempting her rather than rushing in
underprepared.

### Note on difficulty

Patch 0.5.4 lowered pinnacle boss resistances across the board, including Atziri's, alongside
targeted nerfs to a few other pinnacle encounters. If you bounced off this fight in an earlier
patch, it's meaningfully more farmable now.
