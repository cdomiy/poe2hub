## Full Fight Guide

The Arbiter of Ash was PoE2's top pinnacle boss before 0.5.0, and remains mandatory progression,
defeating him is what unlocks the path to the Origin Tower and the current apex fight, Arbiter
of Divinity. He's found at the Burning Monolith inside the Precursor Fortress.

As of 0.5.0, first-time Crisis Fragments are handed out directly through quest versions found in
the Fortress's Western and Eastern Enigma Chambers (defeating Geonor and Doryani respectively),
there's no more relying on Citadel RNG for your very first kill. Subsequent kills still require
finding Crisis Fragments the normal way through Atlas Citadel content.

### Is it worth fighting?

Yes, beyond being mandatory progression toward Arbiter of Divinity, the fragments themselves
are sellable currency even on a bad drop, and the boss's own loot pool includes some of the most
valuable uniques in the game.
