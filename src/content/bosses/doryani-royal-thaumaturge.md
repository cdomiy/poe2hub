## Full Fight Guide

Doryani, Royal Thaumaturge is the final boss of Act 3, fought in the Black Chambers of the Past
after locating the Testing Pit. This is a three-phase escalation: a grounded caster phase, a
mech-mounted melee-range phase, and finally an aerial assault phase once the mech itself is
worn down.

After defeating Doryani's Triumph, Alva escapes her imprisonment and convinces Doryani to join
your side against the Beast. This fight is a major story beat, not just a difficulty check.
