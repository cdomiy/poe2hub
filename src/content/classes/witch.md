## Choosing an Ascendancy

Infernalist is the clear recommended starting point, its fire-and-minion hybrid comes online
during leveling and stays forgiving into endgame. Lich is a strong curse-and-chaos endgame
direction once you have currency behind you, with real group-play value through Unholy Might.
Blood Mage has the highest ceiling of the three but is genuinely difficult to pilot safely,
treat it as a build to grow into rather than a starting point.
