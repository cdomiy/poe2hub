## Choosing an Ascendancy

Stormweaver is the consensus recommended starting Ascendancy regardless of your eventual
endgame target, it levels the smoothest and clears maps fast. Chronomancer is a strategic,
boss-killing specialist that's genuinely weak during the campaign but rewards mastering its
Time Freeze windows at endgame. Disciple of Varashta is the class's summoner option, commanding
permanent Djinn, flexible, but generally rated behind the other two for players chasing pure
damage output.
