## Choosing an Ascendancy

Spirit Walker, added in 0.5.0, is the clear top pick right now, its passive nodes were built
specifically around the Twister combo the class already wants to run, out-scaling the other two
Ascendancies for that mechanic. Amazon is the more beginner-friendly, gear-agnostic leveling
option if you'd rather not commit to Twister immediately. Ritualist has real endgame ceiling
through jewelry scaling, but its gear dependency and downside-first node structure make it a
poor first pick for the class.
