## Choosing an Ascendancy

Titan and Warbringer are both strong, proven league-starter picks, Titan leans purely defensive
with the highest HP ceiling in the game, while Warbringer adds totems and war cries for a more
group-friendly, semi-summoner playstyle. Smith of Kitava trades some offense for the highest raw
survivability in the class, but its custom armour-forging mechanic has a steeper learning curve
and isn't recommended as your very first Warrior build.
