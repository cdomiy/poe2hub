## Choosing an Ascendancy

Witchhunter is frequently rated the strongest all-rounder of the three, mixing burst damage,
party-friendly Culling Strike, and a genuinely strong defensive option in its Sorcery Ward
package. Tactician is the most commonly recommended pick for the well-tested Grenade Ballista
leveling path specifically. Gemling Legionnaire has the highest ceiling and build freedom in the
class, but its currency requirements make it a poor budget or league-starter choice.
