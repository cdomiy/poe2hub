## Choosing an Ascendancy

Shaman is the clear recommended starting point, its Rage-fueled bear-form playstyle has been
demonstrated clearing the full campaign on minimal gear in a fresh SSF run, an unusually strong
signal of a forgiving floor. Oracle is a more niche, less-explored direction built around
foresight and timing mechanics, its lower community coverage reflects genuine niche status
rather than the Ascendancy being non-functional, but it's a harder recommendation for a first
Druid character.
