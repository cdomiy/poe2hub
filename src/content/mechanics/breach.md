## Overview

Breach in Path of Exile 2's 0.5 Return of the Ancients update is a fully reworked endgame mechanic that combines real-time density combat with a unique crafting system (the Genesis Tree) and a multi-stage progression path culminating in the pinnacle boss Xesht, We That Are One. The 0.5.0 rework introduced a dual-bar Stabilise timer, Hive and Fortress sub-encounters, Breachlords Tul and Esh, and the Genesis Tree crafting station at the Monastery of the Keepers hub. After a critical bug fix in 0.5.1 that corrected Unstable Breach density scaling, Breach became one of the top endgame currency farms almost overnight — Catalysts produced through the Genesis Tree sell for significant amounts, and the Hiveblood economy creates a sustainable gambling loop that converts mapping time into raw currency. The Monastery of the Keepers, currently situated to the south of Ziggurat Refuge on the Atlas, serves as Breach's central hub, housing both the Genesis Tree and the passage to the Twisted Domain where Xesht awaits. ## How It Works

**Map-Level Breach** opens a dimensional rift in the map that floods the area with Hiveborn enemies. The encounter runs on a dual-bar timer: a dark bar that always decays toward zero, and a light bar that fills as you kill enemies. Kill fast enough that the light bar outpaces the dark bar's decay, and the combined progress hits 100% — that triggers a **Stabilised Breach**, which spawns a tougher wave of elite enemies followed by the miniboss Vruun, Marshal of Xesht. Defeating Vruun collapses the portal and pays out the full reward: Hiveblood, Breach Splinters, and a chance at a Wombgift. The number of Splinters received depends on the Waystone Tier, how many Breach monsters were slain, how many Clasped Hands were open, and the Map Modifiers that increased Item Quantity. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/breach)

**Breach Progression Path** moves through several stages:

- Collect Breach Splinters from regular map encounters and combine them into a **Breachstone**.
- Use the Breachstone to reveal a **Breach Domain** on the Atlas — a dedicated cluster of maps containing Hive and Fortress content.
- **Hive**: a walled region around a breach. Break through the walls to reach the inner area, then summon Ailith and defend her while she destroys the Hive from within.
- **Fortress**: a larger-scale Hive with progressively harder enemy waves, leading to the Breachlords **Tul and Esh** as its final bosses.
- Beyond the Breachlords lies the true pinnacle: **Xesht**, reached through the High Fortress and the Twisted Domain.

**The Genesis Tree** is Breach's signature crafting system, found at the Monastery of the Keepers. Hiveblood collected from Breach encounters feeds the Genesis Tree, which has four independent branches — Rings, Amulets, Belts, and Currency (Catalysts). Each branch is configured separately (modifier type, minimum modifier level, base type preference) and activated by hatching a Wombgift. The Catalyst branch is the most consistent currency source: Refined Catalysts can sell for hundreds of Exalted Orbs each, and a short session can yield 11-12+ Divines of Catalysts alone. Ten maps at roughly 1,600-1,900 Hiveblood per Breach (on Doryani) nets approximately 60,000 Hiveblood to cash out. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-farming-breach-poe2-currency-farming)

**The Breach Questline** begins by heading to the Monastery of the Keepers. Nearby Maps will be consumed by a Breach Stronghold, and by completing Maps in this area, players unlock additional points on their Breach Atlas Tree. Additionally, 6 points can be acquired by defeating Breach Pinnacle Bosses. The "Tear Open the Rift" Breach Atlas Passive Skill (made multichoice in 0.5.1) allows placing a Breachstone on a section of the Atlas to reveal a new Breach Stronghold with a Hive Colony at the centre leading to Tul and Esh. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/breach)

**Xesht, We That Are One** is Breach's pinnacle boss. After defeating Tul and Esh, you receive a Breachlord Sac. Beside the Genesis Tree is a hidden doorway with an invisible wall that you pass through to find The Dreamer. Behind The Dreamer is a slot where you place the Breachlord Sac, which opens the way to the Twisted Domain, at the end of which you fight Xesht. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/breach)

## Strategy & Tips

- **Prioritize movement and area coverage over pure single-target damage.** Breach rewards builds that can keep killing while repositioning as enemies flood in from multiple directions.
- **Running Breach-boosting Atlas tablets** (particularly ones that add extra rare monsters on Stabilise) multiplies returns significantly once your Atlas passive investment is in place. The 0.5.1 fix specifically made this investment pay off correctly for the first time. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-farming-breach-poe2-currency-farming)
- **Use Doryani as your Atlas Master** for the raw-currency Breach strategy — his cleansed-map chance can drop Fracturing Orbs (around 4-5 Divines each). Alternatively, Jado gives Verisium Remnants a 10% chance to re-roll on resolution. For Hive Blood farming specifically, run three tablets with Hive Blood quantity, ideally with effectiveness and Wombgift quantity. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-farming-breach-poe2-currency-farming)
- **Spend Lavish Wombgifts first on the Catalyst line** (surpassing chance into Refined/jewel Catalysts, no blockers). Once Wombgifts run out, spend remaining Hiveblood on the amulet tree (target Absent amulets) and the ring tree (target Pneumonic rings). Sell Catalysts and crafted bases for consistent Divine income. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-farming-breach-poe2-currency-farming)
- **Prioritize anything that raises the level of Wombgifts** you collect, since level 81-82 gifts roll meaningfully better currency than lower ones. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/gambling-80-000-hiveblood-on-currency-path-of-exile-2)
- **Biome choices matter.** Desert = rare effect / increased rares / rare modifiers (great for Breach). Grass = pack size, spears, summoning circles. Forest = the most increased rare monsters (up to ~65%). City = extra tablet. Mountain = tablet effect, shrines, strongboxes. Water is the weakest for Breach. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/how-to-fully-optimize-your-atlas-tree-in-0-5-path-of-exile-2)
- **If your build struggles with sustained single-target pressure on rares**, the wave-timing at Vruun and beyond will be the harder part of the mechanic, not raw damage.

## Rewards

Breach's reward system centers on the Genesis Tree and its outputs:

- **Catalysts** — the most consistent currency source from Breach. Types include Cold, Lightning, Attack, Life, Defense, Physical, and Spell Catalysts. A single Genesis Tree session can stably produce 3-5 Catalysts, with average earnings of ~20 Chaos per session from Catalysts alone at baseline. Refined Catalysts sell for hundreds of Exalted Orbs each. [(3dmgame.com)](https://m.3dmgame.com/ol/gl/342793.html)
- **Hiveblood** — the primary Breach currency, used to gamble on the Genesis Tree. Approximately 1,600-1,900 Hiveblood per Breach on Doryani; ten maps nets roughly 60,000 Hiveblood. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/the-ultimate-guide-to-farming-breach-poe2-currency-farming)
- **Wombgifts** — dropped from Breach encounters, used to activate Genesis Tree branches. Higher-level Wombgifts (81-82) roll meaningfully better results.
- **Breach Splinters** — combine into a Breachstone to access the Breach Domain and progress toward Tul, Esh, and Xesht.
- **Crafted Rings and Amulets** — the Genesis Tree's Ring and Amulet branches can produce valuable bases like Absent amulets and Pneumonic rings.
- **Xesht Drops** — the pinnacle boss drops unique Breach-themed items and Atlas Passive points.
