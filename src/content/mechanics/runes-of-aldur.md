## Core Loop

Each map spawns a Remnant. Players use Runic Recipes to craft gear in its sockets — different
Runeshape combinations determine which mod categories you end up producing.

## Practical Tips

Prioritize collecting Runeshape combinations that reliably produce defensive mods, and decide
which gear slot you want to craft before entering the map so you don't waste Remnant uses.
