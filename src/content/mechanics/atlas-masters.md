## Overview

The Masters of the Atlas is a secondary Ascendancy-style system introduced in Path of Exile 2's 0.5 Return of the Ancients update that applies directly to whatever map you are about to open, rather than to your character permanently. The system features three Masters — Jado of the Order of the Djinn (Spycraft), Doryani (Science), and Hilda (Hunting) — each offering 12 passive nodes across 4 rows of 3 choices, with players earning up to 4 points total to spend on their tree. You can only have one Master's bonuses active per map, but swapping between Masters costs nothing and can be done freely before opening any new zone. All three Masters progress simultaneously and independently — there is no need to "pick one" permanently. Unlock each row of 3 nodes by performing missions for the Masters. This system layers on top of the Atlas Passive Tree and Precursor Tablet system, providing per-map customization that can dramatically shift your farming outcomes for specific encounters. [(pathofexile.com)](https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540) [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)

## How It Works

### Unlocking the Masters

Each Master has a distinct unlock questline:

- **Jado (Spycraft)** — Unlocked by completing the Sealed Vault in the upper levels of Ziggurat Refuge, then finding and clearing a set of Anomaly Maps (Jade Islands) that Jado sends you to recover artifacts from. You need to complete four Anomaly Maps to fully unlock his tree. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)
- **Doryani (Science)** — Unlocked by clearing Corrupted Nexus areas on the Atlas. Successfully cleansing your first area of Corruption unlocks Doryani and grants the first point. You need to find and cleanse three Corrupted Nexus areas, and upon finding and cleansing the third, Doryani points you toward the Immured Fury Boss, which must be defeated to receive the final point. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)
- **Hilda (Hunting)** — Found at a campsite just before the Withered Willow, slightly southwest of your starting location on the Atlas. She tasks you to hunt a great beast marked on your map. Complete the fight and speak with Hilda to unlock the first row. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)

### Jado — Unique Item and Layout Specialist

Jado's Spycraft tree focuses on Strongbox quality, boss Unique drop chance, and manipulating map layouts. His notable nodes include:

- **In the Wrong Hands** — a guaranteed extra Unique from Powerful map bosses. One of the most popular nodes for Unique farming. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)
- **Unexpected Missions** — Corrupted Waystones open a portal to a completely random map area instead — a free reroll if you land a bad layout.
- **Careful Procurement** — Magic Monster Packs have a 35% chance to have an additional Modifier.
- **Mechanical Guardians** — Non-Unique Strongboxes are guarded by a dormant Construct. These Strongboxes only roll Modifiers which apply to the Construct. Opening the Strongbox activates it. Strongboxes have 2% increased Item Quantity per Modifier.
- **Hidden Patterns** — 10% chance when completing a Map to make nearby Maps accessible.
- **Remnants of Greatness** — Map Bosses have a 20% chance to be guarding a Precursor Terraformer, which can be used to change the biome of a group of maps nearby.
- **Head of the Snake** — Pinnacle Bosses have a 25% chance to drop an additional Unique item. Pinnacle Bosses have a single Revive. Powerful Map Bosses have a 5% chance when defeated to reveal a nearby Citadel. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)

### Doryani — The Generalist

Doryani's Science tree mixes survivability, map density, and loot scaling. He doubles item rarity from Magic monster drops. His notable nodes include:

- **Stitch the Flesh** — grants an extra map Revival, a meaningful safety net for less defensively-solid builds.
- **Improved Calibration** — increases the effect of your Waystone's prefix or suffix modifiers by 25%.
- **Evolutionary Pressure** — 1% increased Rarity of Items found per 100 Power of Monsters slain in the Area. Maps are irradiated. Extra Waystone modifier. Areas are Cleansed or Corrupted. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)
- **Volatile Connection** — 15% chance areas are Cleansed or Corrupted. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)
- **Dangerous Game** — Rogue Exiles have a 20% chance to Flee to an adjacent Map and upgrade their Equipment.
- **Will of the Draíocht** — Azmeri Spirits cannot Possess Rare Monsters.
- **Ancient Inscriptions** — 8% increased effect of Explicit Modifiers on Tablets for each type of Tablet affecting Map area. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)

### Hilda — Pinnacle and Elite Specialist

Hilda's Hunting tree amplifies Rare and Unique Monster difficulty in exchange for proportionally bigger rewards, including a direct bonus to Pinnacle Boss loot. Her notable nodes include:

- **Mighty Prey** — 25% chance to upgrade Map Bosses to Powerful Map Bosses, turning them into bigger fights with better rewards. [(destructoid.com)](https://www.destructoid.com/all-atlas-masters-passives-in-path-of-exile-2-and-best-ones-to-use/)
- **Pinnacle Boss Scavenging (Gutting and Skinning)** — Pinnacle Bosses have a 5% chance to drop an additional Unique item, with Unique Monsters carrying a 1% chance. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)
- **Breeding Season** — 15% increased Rare Monsters. Rare Monsters have 25% increased chance of Monster Modifiers.
- **Patient Battue** — 5% chance 3 additional Rare Monsters are replaced by a random Map Boss. 25% chance 1 Rare Monster is replaced by a random Map Boss. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)
- **Scarred Lands** — modifies map terrain and monster spawns (specific effects vary by tier).
- **Claimed Territories** — Overlord's Influence and Overlord's Domain Atlas Passive Skills have 45% chance to add twice as much content. [(mobalytics.gg)](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/guides/atlas-tree-ascendancy)

## Strategy & Tips

- **Match the Master to what you are about to do, not a permanent build choice.** Hilda before a pinnacle attempt, Jado when you have stacked a map with Strongboxes, Doryani when exploring unknown Atlas territory or when you need the extra revive to survive a dangerous map.
- **A commonly recommended baseline setup** combines In the Wrong Hands (Jado), Stitch the Flesh and Improved Calibration (Doryani), and Mighty Prey with Patient Battue (Hilda) — a mix of safety net, loot amplification, and Unique hunting that works reasonably well regardless of what you are specifically farming.
- **All three Masters progress simultaneously and independently.** Unlock all three over time and rotate based on your current farming target. There is no opportunity cost to unlocking a Master you are not currently using.
- **Early on, before your build feels fully solid, Doryani's survivability nodes are the safer default.** The extra revive from Stitch the Flesh can be the difference between completing a dangerous map and losing progress. Add Hilda's boss-amplifying nodes once you are confident you can handle the extra difficulty they bring.
- **For Breach farming, Doryani is recommended** for the cleansed-map chance that can drop Fracturing Orbs. For Expedition Grand Expeditions, Jado gives Verisium Remnants a 10% chance to re-roll on resolution. For standard Expedition maps, switch to Hilda for increased Lair Monsters. [(exile.codex-wiki.com)](https://exile.codex-wiki.com/guides/expedition-poe2-0-5-return-of-the-ancients)
- **Ancient Inscriptions (Doryani)** is exceptionally strong when you are running multiple Tablet types on overlapping Towers, since it scales with each type of Tablet affecting the map area.
- **Head of the Snake (Jado)** provides a single Revive for Pinnacle Bosses — valuable insurance on high-stakes fights, plus the 25% chance for an extra Unique drop from Pinnacle Bosses.

## Rewards

The Masters of the Atlas system does not generate independent rewards — instead, it amplifies the rewards from every other system:

- **Jado** increases Unique item drops (In the Wrong Hands, Head of the Snake), improves Strongbox quality, and can reroll map layouts (Unexpected Missions) or change biomes (Remnants of Greatness).
- **Doryani** provides safety (Stitch the Flesh extra Revive), amplifies Waystone modifier effects (Improved Calibration), boosts item rarity from Magic monsters, and can drop Fracturing Orbs from Cleansed maps (Evolutionary Pressure).
- **Hilda** directly increases boss reward quality (Mighty Prey, Gutting and Skinning), replaces Rare monsters with Map Bosses (Patient Battue), and increases Rare Monster density and modifiers (Breeding Season).
