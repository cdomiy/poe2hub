## Core loop

Using a Logbook unveils a section of ocean southeast of your Atlas, filled with islands to
sail between. On each island, you place explosives to dig up buried Kalguuran ruins (Remnants),
then fight off waves of monsters that spawn to defend them. Successfully clearing a Remnant
rewards Verisium, runic alloys, and runes, the core currency of the Runeforging system, plus a
chance at raw Divine Orbs.

## Standard vs. Grand Expedition

- **Standard Expedition** is the low-investment entry point, a reliable way to print Divine
  Orbs even on white maps with minimal Atlas tree commitment.
- **Grand Expedition** (the direct successor to the old "Logbook" encounters) is the high-ceiling
  version, requiring real Atlas tree investment but scaling much further, Remnant count now
  scales with your Waystone tier, maxing out at Tier 15+.

## Faction Leaders

Four Faction Leaders, **Olroth**, **Medved**, **Vorana**, and **Uhtred**, can be found and
fought across the various islands you discover, each tied to their own rewards.

## Practical tips

- If you're not finding Logbooks naturally, a specific boss now guarantees one on defeat, you
  can no longer get fully locked out of the mechanic through bad luck.
- The "Feeling Lucky?" Atlas node unlocks Liquid Verisium from Farrow, letting you reroll a
  Remnant's rune options instead of accepting whatever it reveals by default, useful if you're
  hunting a specific rune type rather than taking whatever comes up.
- Grand Expedition chest rewards were substantially reworked in 0.5.3, expect currency, unique
  items, and Waystones rather than the basic gear drops from earlier patches.
